# Inspect AI

## What it is
A structured evaluation framework for LLMs from the UK AI Safety Institute. Supports sandboxed tasks, multi-turn dialogues, and scoring.

## Distinctive traits
- Declarative task definitions with strong sandboxing support.
- Built-in eval metrics and model-graded scoring.
- Designed for safety and capability evaluation rather than production agents.
