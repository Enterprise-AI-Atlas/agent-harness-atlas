# Camunda AI Agent

## What it is
Camunda's AI agent integration brings LLM-driven tasks into BPMN process orchestration. Processes coordinate agents as BPMN service tasks or workers.

## Distinctive traits
- BPMN-driven orchestration with human tasks and timers.
- Persistent process state as a first-class concept.
- Strong governance, audit, and long-running workflow support.
